What the meanings of the virtues

Courage
refers to the fact that one should not be afraid of the consequences of an action if he or she knows that it is the right thing to do
Assertiveness
is the quality of being self-assured and confident without being aggressive to defend a right point of view or a relevant statement.
Respect
is a positive feeling or deferential action shown towards someone or something considered important or held in high esteem or regard.
Justice
Giving to others what is rightfully theirs constitutes justice.
Kindeness
the quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate.
